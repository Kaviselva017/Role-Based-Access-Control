delivering products or services to customers by understanding their
needs and creating value. Based on marketing datasets such as customer
behavior, campaign performance, and sales trends, marketing involves
strategies like branding, advertising, and communication to attract and
retain customers. It plays a critical role in driving revenue growth and
building long-term customer relationships.

🔹 Key Details:

Focuses on customer needs Uses campaigns and data Drives sales and
engagement

🔹 Insight: Effective marketing combines data analysis with creative
strategies.

11\. What is conversion rate?

🔹 Answer: Conversion rate is the percentage of users who complete a
desired action, such as making a purchase, signing up, or clicking a
link, out of the total number of visitors. Marketing datasets like
website traffic and conversions are used to calculate this metric. It is
a key performance indicator that measures how effectively a campaign
turns visitors into customers.

🔹 Key Details:

Formula: Conversions ÷ Visitors Measures campaign effectiveness Based on
analytics data

🔹 Insight: Higher conversion rates indicate better targeting and user
experience.

12\. What is click-through rate (CTR)?

🔹 Answer: Click-through rate (CTR) measures the percentage of users who
click on an ad or link after seeing it. It is calculated as clicks
divided by impressions. Marketing datasets such as ad campaign
performance include CTR as a key metric to evaluate engagement and ad
effectiveness.

🔹 Key Details:

Formula: Clicks ÷ Impressions Measures engagement Used in ads and emails

🔹 Insight: High CTR indicates compelling content and targeting.
